The App

Find sessions around you

Sport With Me allow you to see all public sport session around you created by other users. You can apply search filter based on date, sport, duration…

Select a session

You can easly select a session and get a preview.

If you like the session you can join it.

Create your own session

When creating a session you indicate

  • the name of the session
  • the location
  • the date
  • the duration
  • the sport
  • the maximum number of people
  • the privacy (a private session won’t appear in the map view of other users)

Private session

You wanna a organize a sport session with your friends?

Just create private session and invit your friends to the session. Only invited users can join a private session.

Public session

You are alone and you wanna pratice sport with other people to keep you motivated?

Juste create a public session and people around the area will be able to join your session.

You wanna a play football or volley, but your friends and yourself are not enough to from tow teams.

Create a public session, invit your friends and let other people join the session to fill the gaps.

Your sessions

Once you created your session it appears in your session list

Create your own sessions

For each of your sessions you can get details givinng you an overview of the session.

You can see:

  • The name of the session
  • The sport
  • The date
  • The duration
  • The maximum number of people
  • The number of teams
  • The number of participants
  • The place of the session
In a session you can also invit a another users just by typing his username

You can also choose to delete or leave the session.

Wanna go to the session quick? Get a direct itenerary to the session with one button.

Team mangement

In a session you can create teams and easily assign other participants to a particular team.

You can easily create or delete teams, move users to differents teams.
